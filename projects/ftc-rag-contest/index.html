Fair Trade Commission AI & Data Utilization Contest — RAG Pipeline

A legal-document RAG pipeline designed around hybrid retrieval, RRF fusion, reranking, and metric-driven improvement loops.

Problem Context

Decision documents require exact legal references and semantically relevant context at the same time. The challenge was to improve retrieval quality while keeping the generation stage aligned with the contest-provided metrics.

What I Did

  • Designed a hybrid retrieval strategy combining dense retrieval, sparse signals for legal-clause precision, and multi-vector retrieval.
  • Used RRF fusion and reranking to reduce noise in top-ranked chunks.
  • Separated retrieval-stage and generation-stage metrics to identify where performance degraded.
  • Aligned experiments with contest-provided evaluation metrics including Recall@5, MRR, BERTScore, and F1.

Outcome and Portfolio Value

  • Improved the explainability of the RAG pipeline by making retrieval and generation bottlenecks visible.
  • Built a practical example of metric-driven RAG optimization for public-sector documents.
  • Prepared a strong interview story around evaluation design, retrieval strategy, and legal-document constraints.
